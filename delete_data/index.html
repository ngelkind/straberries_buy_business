User Data Deletion

Effective date: February 15, 2026

If you used Meta/Facebook login or interacted with us through Meta platforms and want your data deleted, follow the steps below.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send a deletion request by WhatsApp or phone to +972 55-264-9363.
  2. Include identifying details used with our service (for example your phone number and full name).
  3. Write "Data Deletion Request" in your message.

What We Delete

  • Contact and order-related information associated with your request, where legally permitted.
  • Any stored profile details received through Meta integrations, if applicable.

What May Be Retained

We may retain limited data when required by law, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or other legitimate legal obligations.

Processing Time

We aim to complete valid deletion requests within 30 days of verification.

Confirmation

After processing your request, we will send confirmation through the same communication channel you used.

Questions

For questions related to data deletion, contact us at +972 55-264-9363.
